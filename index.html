James Bond is a global phenomenon.
He is one of the most iconic, fictional characters in the world.
He is 007 licence to kill, an international secret agent working for M16 in England.
Calling him a 'world famous secret agent' seems a contradiction of sorts.

Actors that have played Bond include Sean Connery, Roger Moore, Pierce Brosnan and Daniel Craig.

The author Ian Fleming

James Bond was created by the British award winning author Ian Fleming.
Ian was born in London, England in 1908.
Throughout his career he took on many roles in the Royal Navy and also worked as a Journalist and Stockbroker.
He also wrote the beloved children's novel Chitty Chitty Bang Bang.
He died in 1964 aged 54.
